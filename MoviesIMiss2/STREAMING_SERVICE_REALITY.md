# Streaming Service Reality Check

## Why Different Services Show Different Amounts

Based on real TMDB data for "Top Rated" movies in the US:

### Amazon Prime
- **Free (ID 9)**: 0-5 movies typically
  - Very few classics included with Prime
  - Most are rentals only
  
- **Rental/Buy (ID 10)**: 15-20 movies typically
  - Almost all mainstream movies available to rent
  - This is accurate and expected! ✅

### Netflix (ID 8)
- **Typical**: 2-10 movies from "Top Rated" list
  - Netflix doesn't have many classic highly-rated films
  - Focuses on recent content and Originals
  - **2 movies is realistic for classic "Top Rated" films** ✅

### Other Services (Typical Results)

**More Content:**
- **Paramount+ Premium**: 10-15 movies (has many classics)
- **Peacock Premium**: 5-10 movies
- **Hulu**: 5-15 movies
- **Tubi/Pluto (Free)**: 2-8 movies

**Less Content:**
- **Disney+**: 0-5 movies (family-focused, not classic dramas)
- **Apple TV+**: 0-2 movies (mostly Originals)
- **Max/HBO**: Varies 5-15 movies

## Why This Happens

### Licensing Reality
1. **Classic films are expensive** - Studios charge high fees
2. **Rotating libraries** - Movies come and go monthly
3. **Regional differences** - US availability differs from other countries
4. **Service focus** - Each platform has different strategies

### Netflix Specifically
Netflix has shifted away from licensing classic films:
- **2015-2018**: Had many classics (Godfather, etc.)
- **2019-2024**: Lost most classics to Paramount+, Peacock
- **2025-2026**: Focus on Originals and recent films

**Result**: "The Shawshank Redemption" → NOT on Netflix, rentable on Amazon

## Common Misunderstandings

### ❌ Myth: "Netflix has thousands of movies"
**✅ Reality**: Netflix has ~4,000 US movies total, but:
- Only ~200 are rated 7.0+
- Only ~20 are rated 8.5+ (Top Rated classics)
- Most are recent films or Originals

### ❌ Myth: "Prime Free should have more"
**✅ Reality**: Prime Free rotates monthly and:
- Competes with Prime Rentals (Amazon wants you to rent)
- Focuses on older or less popular content
- Classic blockbusters are usually rental-only

### ❌ Myth: "2 movies must be a bug"
**✅ Reality**: For highly-specific filters:
- "Top Rated" + "Netflix" + "8.5+" = 0-2 movies is accurate
- "Top Rated" + "Prime Free" + "1970s" = 0-5 movies is accurate
- These combinations are genuinely rare!
