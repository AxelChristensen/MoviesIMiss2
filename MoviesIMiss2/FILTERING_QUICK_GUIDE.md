# Quick Guide: Filtering by Streaming Service

## How to Filter Movies by Where You Can Watch Them

### Step 1: Open the Browse Tab
Navigate to the Browse tab to see the curated movie list.

### Step 2: Select a Streaming Service
Tap the **streaming service menu** in the toolbar (shows "All Services" by default).

Choose from:
- **All Services** - No filtering
- **Netflix**
- **Amazon Prime Video** ⭐ 
- **Disney+**
- **Hulu**
- **Max (HBO Max)**
- **Apple TV+**
- **Peacock**
- **Paramount+**
- **Tubi TV** (Free with ads)
- **Pluto TV** (Free with ads)

### Step 3: Enable "Free Only" (Optional)
**Important for Amazon Prime Video users!**

Tap the **"Free Only"** button to:
- ✅ Show movies **included with your subscription**
- ✅ Show **free ad-supported** content
- ❌ Hide movies only available to **rent**
- ❌ Hide movies only available to **buy**

### Example: Finding Movies Free on Prime

1. Select **"Amazon Prime Video"** from the menu
2. Enable **"Free Only"** toggle
3. Browse shows **only movies included with Prime membership**
4. Movies available only as Prime Video rentals are **hidden**

### Step 4: Combine with Other Filters

You can use streaming filters **alongside**:
- **Decade filters** (1980s, 1990s, etc.)
- **Rating filters** (8.0+, 8.5+, etc.)
- **Actor filters** (Tom Hanks, Meryl Streep, etc.)
- **List types** (Top Rated, Popular, etc.)

Example combinations:
- "Netflix" + "2010s" + "8.0+" = Highly-rated recent Netflix movies
- "Prime Video" + "Free Only" + "Tom Hanks" = Tom Hanks movies included with Prime
- "Hulu" + "Horror" = Horror movies on Hulu

### Step 5: Reset Filters

Tap **"Reset Filters"** to:
- Clear all filters including streaming service
- Return to showing all movies
- Clear the provider cache

## Understanding the Results

### What You'll See
- Movies available on your selected service
- In the movie details, you'll see provider logos
- Link to JustWatch for complete streaming info

### What Gets Filtered
- Only movies with confirmed provider availability are shown
- Movies **without** provider data are **excluded** (waiting for data to load)
- Shows loading indicator while fetching streaming availability
- Empty results mean no movies match your criteria

### Performance Notes
- First load may take a moment (fetching provider data)
- Subsequent filtering is instant (data is cached)
- Cache clears when you reset filters or change categories

## Tips & Tricks

### Finding Free Content
Enable "Free Only" without selecting a specific service to see:
- Movies free on **any** streaming service
- Both subscription-included and ad-supported options
- Great for discovering what you can watch right now

### Prime Video Users
Prime Video lists both:
- **Included content** (free with membership)
- **Rental/purchase options** (paid)

Use "Free Only" to see **only** what's included with Prime!

### Multiple Services
Currently supports one service at a time. To check multiple:
1. Filter by Service A
2. Note interesting movies
3. Filter by Service B
4. Compare results

### Availability May Vary
- Provider data is for **US region** by default
- Some movies may not have provider information
- Availability changes over time
- Check movie details for current streaming options

## Troubleshooting

### No Results?
- Try disabling "Free Only"
- Check if the service has content in your selected decade/genre
- Reset all filters and try again
- Some filters are very restrictive when combined

### Missing Movies?
- Provider data may not be available for all movies
- Very new or very old movies may lack provider info
- Try viewing in "All Services" mode

### Slow Loading?
- First-time provider loading fetches data for all movies
- This is normal and only happens once per browsing session
- Subsequent filtering is instant

## Common Use Cases

### "What can I watch on Netflix tonight?"
1. Select **Netflix**
2. Optionally add filters (decade, rating, actor)
3. Browse the filtered results

### "What's free on Prime Video?"
1. Select **Amazon Prime Video**
2. Enable **"Free Only"**
3. See only Prime-included content

### "Show me highly-rated movies on Hulu"
1. Select **Hulu**
2. Select rating filter **"8.0+"**

### "What Tom Hanks movies can I stream?"
1. Select actor **"Tom Hanks"**
2. Enable **"Free Only"** 
3. See Tom Hanks movies available on any streaming service
